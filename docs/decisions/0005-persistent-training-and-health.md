# 0005 — Persistent training and health

## Decision

Training is configured as a persistent plan instead of a repeated daily action. Advancing the day resolves the selected focus automatically.

Health is represented by readiness, load, soreness, pain, injury risk and medical availability rather than one health bar.

## Reason

The game needs meaningful preparation without becoming a repetitive clicker. Development, availability and depth-chart position must be connected.

## Consequences

- training choices create long-term development bias;
- aggressive work can produce faster gains and higher risk;
- recovery has an opportunity cost but protects later sessions;
- old version 4 saves receive a deterministic initial training state through migration 4 → 5.
