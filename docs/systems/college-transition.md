# College transition

Version 0.11 closes the high-school vertical slice and moves a signed athlete into the first college orientation.

## Final decision

A formal signing is only available after the high-school season is complete. A program must provide one of two legitimate entry routes:

- full athletic scholarship;
- preferred walk-on place backed by active staff contact and a verified evaluation.

The game does not generate a rescue offer when no valid route exists. A verbal commitment must be withdrawn before signing another program.

## Offseason simulation

Reporting to campus advances the calendar to August 9, 2027 and resolves the graduate semester and offseason from existing state:

- discipline and health;
- position coach development;
- current medical clearance;
- academics;
- physical profile;
- deterministic career seed.

The result changes overall rating, body weight, GPA, health and confidence. The simulation can be strong, average or poor; it is not a guaranteed progression reward.

## Promise reality

The college position room is materialized only after the athlete arrives. The actual ranking combines player quality, experience, program prestige, depth competition, youth opportunity and the clarity of the role promised during recruiting.

The game compares the promised role with the actual room and records one of three verdicts:

- kept;
- uncertain;
- misleading.

## First-year priority

During orientation the player chooses one locked priority:

- compete for reps immediately;
- learn the system;
- build an academic base.

The decision is applied once to prevent repeated bonus farming. The complete college weekly loop starts in the next development phase.
