# Training and health

Patch 0.5 adds a persistent football training plan and a body-readiness model.

## Player decision

The player selects one active focus and one intensity. The choice persists until changed and is resolved automatically when a game day advances.

Focuses:

- position craft;
- explosive power;
- film and install;
- recovery reset.

## Body state

The simulation tracks readiness, acute load, chronic load, soreness, pain, injury risk, medical status and an optional active health issue.

Readiness affects session quality. Load spikes, soreness, pain, fatigue, medical quality and training intensity affect injury risk. Random checks use the career seed and game date.

## Development

Each focus has separate multipliers for technique, athleticism, football IQ and competitiveness. High ratings have diminishing returns. Training quality, body readiness, medical availability and the current weekly schedule modify gains.

## UI rule

The main Today screen only shows readiness, current focus and urgent restrictions. Training controls live in a compact subtab. Detailed body and medical data open in a scrollable bottom sheet.
