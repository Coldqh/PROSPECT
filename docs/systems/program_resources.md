# Program resources

Patch 15 makes institutional resources finite and causal.

Each active program owns a deterministic resource profile:

- annual and football budgets;
- coaching, recruiting, medical, facilities and academic allocations;
- NIL capacity;
- donor confidence and board patience;
- current operating balance and financial pressure;
- committed recruiting, medical and NIL spending;
- infrastructure, medicine and academic-support quality.

Resources affect simulation rather than only UI:

- medical investment changes recovery speed and injury exposure;
- facilities and coaching investment change player development;
- recruiting and NIL capacity limit commitments and transfers;
- coaching resources affect hiring and retention;
- deficits increase pressure and can force budget cuts;
- strong results and donor confidence can expand the next annual budget.

The system is deterministic for a stable seed and world state. Annual resource plans are recalculated during winter evaluation and are preserved in save data.
